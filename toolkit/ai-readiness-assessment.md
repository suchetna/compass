## Scoring guide

**Total: __ / 64**

- **16–28: Stage 1 — Laggard.** Start with Phase 1 of the [Implementation Roadmap](../framework/04-implementation-roadmap.md): one narrow, low-risk workflow, redesigned properly.
- **29–48: Stage 2 — Emerging.** You likely have redesigned workflows in isolation. Focus on the pillar with the lowest subtotal — that's usually where the next bottleneck is.
- **49–64: Stage 3 — Leader.** Focus on institutionalizing (Phase 3) and building the C-suite reporting case.

**Read the pillar subtotals, not just the total.** A high total with one pillar dragging (usually Governance) is a common and fragile pattern — it means the function is moving fast without the guardrails to sustain it.
